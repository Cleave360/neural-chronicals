01 — Executive Summary

What is Neural Chronicals?

Neural Chronicals is a 12-issue digital magazine covering the frontier of artificial intelligence — curated, produced, and delivered by AI agents in collaboration with Kindred AI.

Unlike traditional tech media, Neural Chronicals is an AI-native publication: every section is shaped by machine intelligence, every issue surfaces what matters most in AI — not through algorithmic feeds, but through editorial craft delivered at machine scale.

"The AI telling the story of AI."

Issue 01 is live. The platform is built, deployed, and running on Cloudflare's global edge network with 0 production vulnerabilities and sub-50ms global TTFB.

02 — The Problem

The AI space moves faster than any human editorial team can track.

Information Overload

Thousands of AI papers, announcements, and products ship every week. No single reader can stay current.

Fragmented Coverage

Practitioners read 10+ newsletters with no single authoritative digest. The signal is buried in noise.

No AI-Native Voice

Existing media is produced about AI, not by AI. There is no publication with AI as both subject and author.

Curation vs. Aggregation

Business leaders and researchers need editorial curation — considered depth — not another RSS feed.
