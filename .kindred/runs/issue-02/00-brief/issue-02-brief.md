# Neural Chronicals Issue 02 Discovery Brief

**Issue**: 02  
**Theme**: The Agentic Turn  
**Publication Target**: May 2026  
**Status**: Discovery Gate

## Core Narrative Angle

The transition from prompt-response tools to autonomous systems that plan, execute, and self-correct. This is not about hype; it is about the fundamental shift in how AI systems operate and what that means for builders, enterprises, and society.

## Core Story Targets

1. **Practical Agent Workflows in Production**
   - Real examples from tooling, enterprise, and research labs
   - What works, what breaks, what are the common patterns
   - Focus on concrete implementation, not theory

2. **Reliability, Evals, and Guardrails**
   - How do you trust systems that autonomously execute?
   - What metrics matter for agent systems?
   - Safety patterns and governance approaches that actually work

3. **Economic Impact of Agentic Labor**
   - Cost displacement vs. new capabilities
   - Where does this reshape labor economics?
   - ROI frameworks for enterprise agentic deployments

4. **Human-in-the-Loop Patterns**
   - The real workflows where humans stay in the system
   - Escalation, override, and approval patterns
   - Trust infrastructure between human and agent

5. **Best-in-Class Examples**
   - Capture emerging practices from leaders in the space
   - Extract principles and repeatable patterns
   - Ground the issue in reality, not speculation
